ABOUT

We are IoTitude
a virtual company that started working on IoTcity 2.0 project at WIMMA lab on May 15th 2017.

We consist of seven students from JAMK University of Applied Sciences.

Our customer wanted us to develop an IoT-solution for study purposes mainly – one that could handle large amounts of various data and would be stable, tested and secure.

SOLUTION

IoTcity 2.0

The open doors day, an event when WIMMA lab was open for companies to come see our progress and talk with future developers, was held June 9th 2017.
Our goal was to make a simple, functional demo product for this event.

We created a physical IoT-device called "Harmitusmittari" that collects the status of peoples’ current mood with an application that has a button for four different moods. We collected data of peoples' impressions on the event.
From Harmitusmittari the data went through Kaa into Cassandra. From Cassandra the data was visualized on a web page with Zeppelin.
